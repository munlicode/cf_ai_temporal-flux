# Why Cloudflare

When I applied, I was so hyped, but I did not know what I learned afterwards. Yesterday, I had an insight and it became much more clear what it means when you are asked 'Why Cloudflare?'.

## Before

In my cover letter that added to application, _I wrote honestly_, however, I believe that it _does not fully cover_ the whole extend of what I wanted to say. I told about the fact that **my ambition** is to _contribute to a future that goes beyond global scale_, for example _space networking_. That was one of the reasons why cloudflare. There is more to it, and that is why I am adding writing this.

## Now

However, after yesterday, when I was a bit too much in my thoughts, I had remembered [Conversation between Simon Sinek and Gary Vee](https://soundcloud.com/garyvee/simon-sinek-your-why-vs-the-companys-why-always-being-yourself-askgaryvee-episode-226?in=nurzhan-muratkhan-104322128/sets/endlose-denkweise). Simon was telling that he asks people of _what they want from him_, not smart people, because there are many companies with smart people.

I kind of discovered an area, I was not aware of, and the reason why it is _more important_ to _understand yourself to deepest levels_.

So getting back to the topic, I had a bit of reflection and came to the thought that **what I truly from Cloudflare is** _opportunity to talk, build together and learn hands-on from people, who are building secure, fast and affordable internet._ _If I was to learn from those people, I will be able to create more value and shape better future of both me and world's._ Anyone can do that alone and it is great way to start, but at some point I realized that there is more benefit if we do that together, because _we can assist each other._

I just thought of _what I can take personally_, not only that they serve a mission, because there are a lot of companies with their own missions. So far, I can tell that I am fascinated by how people in Cloudflare connect everything.

For example, pricing model that is so _simple_, but yet allows _high profits_ for the company.

Let's stop here for now.

If you read this far, it means you have an _opportunity to listen to podcast or get a bit from my insights_.
